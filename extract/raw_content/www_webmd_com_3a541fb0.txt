1 of 12 /  Overview
-------------------

 [View All](https://www.webmd.com/breast-cancer/guide-chapter-breast-cancer-overview) ![]()

* [### What Is Breast Cancer?
  
  Breast cancer is when the cells under one or both breasts grow out of control.](https://www.webmd.com/breast-cancer/understanding-breast-cancer-basics)
* [### How Fast Does Breast Cancer Grow?
  
  If you have breast cancer, you may wonder how fast the cancer is growing and how fast it will continue to grow.](https://www.webmd.com/breast-cancer/breast-cancer-how-fast-grows)
* [### Breast Cancer Survival Rates
  
  With breast cancer, your focus will be on getting the right treatment. You'll also wonder about your chances of surviving.](https://www.webmd.com/breast-cancer/breast-cancer-survival-rates)
* [### Breast Cancer in Young Women
  
  Under 7% of all breast cancer cases happen in women under 40. But it can happen at any age.](https://www.webmd.com/breast-cancer/breast-cancer-young-women)

3 of 12 /  Risks, Prevention & Screening
----------------------------------------

 [View All](https://www.webmd.com/breast-cancer/guide-chapter-breast-cancer-risks-prevention) ![]()

* [### What Causes Breast Cancer?
  
  The cause of breast cancer is unclear, but we know the main risks: being older and having breast cancer in your family.](https://www.webmd.com/breast-cancer/what-causes-breast-cancer)
* [### Breast Lumps
  
  You notice that something is different with your breast, and you find a lump. Get it checked by a doctor, but don't panic.](https://www.webmd.com/breast-cancer/benign-breast-lumps)
* [### What Are Genetic Breast Cancer Mutations?
  
  Chances are, your breast cancer isn't caused by a faulty gene. But in about 5% to 10% of cases, the cause is hereditary.](https://www.webmd.com/breast-cancer/genetic-mutations)
* [### Lifestyle Factors that Raise Breast Cancer Risk
  
  Some lifestyle factors may raise your chances for breast cancer. Having these traits doesn’t mean you’ll get the disease.](https://www.webmd.com/breast-cancer/cm/breast-cancer-lifestyle-factors)
 [View All](https://www.webmd.com/breast-cancer/guide-chapter-breast-cancer-risks-prevention) 

4 of 12 /  Tests & Diagnosis
----------------------------

 [View All](https://www.webmd.com/breast-cancer/guide-chapter-breast-cancer-tests-diagnosis) ![]()

* [### Breast Biopsy
  
  A breast biopsy is a procedure in which your doctor takes out cells or a small piece of tissue from part of your breast.](https://www.webmd.com/breast-cancer/breast-biopsy)
* [### HER2 FISH Testing for Breast Cancer
  
  The FISH (fluorescence in situ hybridization) test checks the DNA of cancer cells for extra copies of the HER2/neu gene.](https://www.webmd.com/breast-cancer/her2-fish-test-for-breast-cancer)
* [### Oncotype DX Test for Breast Cancer
  
  The Oncotype DX analyzes a sample of cancer tumor to see the activity of certain genes that affect the cancer's outcome.](https://www.webmd.com/breast-cancer/oncotype-dx-test-breast-cancer)
* [### Immunohistochemistry Test
  
  The immunohistochemistry (IHC) test can tell your oncologist whether you have certain types of breast cancer.](https://www.webmd.com/breast-cancer/breast-cancer-immunohistochemistry-test)
 [View All](https://www.webmd.com/breast-cancer/guide-chapter-breast-cancer-tests-diagnosis) 

5 of 12 /  Types
----------------

 [View All](https://www.webmd.com/breast-cancer/guide-chapter-breast-cancer-types) ![]()

* [### Types of Breast Cancer
  
  About 80% of all breast cancers are “ER-positive.” About 65% of these are also “PR-positive.”](https://www.webmd.com/breast-cancer/breast-cancer-types-er-positive-her2-positive)
* [### Inflammatory Breast Cancer
  
  Inflammatory breast cancer is a rare, aggressive form of breast cancer that often appears as a rash or skin irritation.](https://www.webmd.com/breast-cancer/inflammatory-breast-cancer)
* [### Invasive vs. Metastatic Breast Cancer
  
  Invasive and metastatic both refer to cancer that has spread from the site in the breast where it first started.](https://www.webmd.com/breast-cancer/invasive-vs-metastatic-breast-cancer)
* [### Invasive Lobular Carcinoma
  
  Invasive lobular carcinoma is breast cancer that begins in one of the milk glands and spreads to other parts of the breast.](https://www.webmd.com/breast-cancer/lobular-carcinoma-invasive-and-in-situ)
 [View All](https://www.webmd.com/breast-cancer/guide-chapter-breast-cancer-types)
